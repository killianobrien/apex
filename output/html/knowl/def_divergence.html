Definition 15.2.9. Divergence of a Vector Field.

The divergence of a vector field \(\vec F\) is
\begin{equation*} \divv \vec F = \nabla \cdot \vec F\text{.} \end{equation*}
  • In the plane, with \(\vec F = \langle M,N\rangle\text{,}\) \(\divv \vec F = M_x+N_y\text{.}\)
  • In space, with \(\vec F = \langle M,N,P\rangle\text{,}\) \(\divv \vec F = M_x+N_y+P_z\text{.}\)
