In the following exercises, a planar curve \(C\) is given along with a surface \(f\) that is defined over \(C\text{.}\) Set up the line integral \(\ds \int_Cf(s)\, ds\text{,}\) then approximate its value using technology.
11.
\(C\) is the portion of the parabola \(y=2x^2+x+1\) on \([0,1]\text{;}\) the surface is \(f(x,y)=x^2+2y\text{.}\)
12.
\(C\) is the portion of the curve \(y=\sin x\) on \([0,\pi]\text{;}\) the surface is \(f(x,y)=x\text{.}\)
13.
\(C\) is the ellipse given by \(\vec r(t) = \langle 2\cos t,\sin t\rangle\) on \([0,2\pi]\text{;}\) the surface is \(f(x,y)=10-x^2-y^2\text{.}\)
14.
\(C\) is the portion of \(y=x^3\) on \([-1,1]\text{;}\) the surface is \(f(x,y)=2x+3y+5\text{.}\)
