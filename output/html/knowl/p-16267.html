We now compute \(\iiint_D \divv \vec F\, dV\text{.}\) We can describe \(D\) as the domain bounded by (think “surface to surface, curve to curve, point to point”):
\begin{equation*} 0\leq z\leq 1-x^2-y^2, -\sqrt{1-x^2}\leq y\leq \sqrt{1-x^2}, -1\leq x\leq 1\text{.} \end{equation*}
