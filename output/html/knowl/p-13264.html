Alternate notations for \(f_x(x,y)\) include:
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}f(x,y),\,\,\frac{\pf}{\px},\,\,\frac{\pz}{\px},\,\,\text{ and } \,z_x\text{,} \end{equation*}
with similar notations for \(f_y(x,y)\text{.}\) For ease of notation, \(f_x(x,y)\) is often abbreviated \(f_x\text{.}\)
