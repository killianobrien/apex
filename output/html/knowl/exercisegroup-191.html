Exercise Group 7–12.
In the following exercises, find a formula for the \(n\)th term of the Taylor series of \(f(x)\text{,}\) centered at \(c\text{,}\) by finding the coefficients of the first few powers of \(x\) and looking for a pattern. (The formulas for several of these are found in Key Idea 9.8.11; show work verifying these formula.)
7.
\(f(x) = \cos(x)\text{;}\)\(c=\pi/2\)
8.
\(f(x) = 1/x\text{;}\)\(c=1\)
9.
\(f(x) = e^{-x}\text{;}\)\(c=0\)
10.
\(f(x) = \ln(1+x)\text{;}\)\(c=0\)
11.
\(f(x) = x/(x+1)\text{;}\)\(c=1\)
12.
\(f(x) = \sin(x)\text{;}\)\(c=\pi/4\)
