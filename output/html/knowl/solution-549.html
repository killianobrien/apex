Solution 7.2.20.1.
Orient the cone such that the tip is at the origin and the \(x\)-axis is perpendicular to the base. The cross-sections of this cone are right, isosceles triangles with side length \(2x/5\text{;}\) thus the cross-sectional areas are \(A(x) = 2x^2/25\text{,}\) giving a volume of \(80/3\) units\(^3\text{.}\)
