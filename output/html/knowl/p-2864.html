Be careful to understand that this theorem states “Relative extrema on open intervals occur at critical points.” It does not say “All critical numbers produce relative extrema.” For instance, consider \(f(x) = x^3\text{.}\) Since \(\fp(x) = 3x^2\text{,}\) it is straightforward to determine that \(x=0\) is a critical number of \(f\text{.}\) However, \(f\) has no relative extrema, as illustrated in Figure 3.1.17.
