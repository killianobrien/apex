Solution 12.3.3.42.1.
The position function of the ball is \(\vrt = \la (v_0\cos(\theta) )t,-16t^2+(v_0\sin(\theta) )t+6\ra\text{,}\) where \(\theta\) is the angle of elevation and \(v_0\) is the initial ball speed.
  1. With \(v_0 = 73.33\) ft/s, there are two angles of elevation possible. An angle of \(\theta = 9.47^\circ\) delivers the ball in 0.83 s, while an angle of \(79.57^\circ\) delivers the ball in \(4.5\) s.
  2. With \(\theta=8^\circ\text{,}\) the initial speed must be 53.8 mph\(\approx 78.9\) ft/s.
