Let \(\vec u\) and \(\vec v\) be nonzero vectors in \(\mathbb{R}^2\) or \(\mathbb{R}^3\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \dotp uv = \norm{\vec u}\,\norm{\vec v} \cos(\theta)\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(\theta\text{,}\) \(0\leq\theta\leq \pi\text{,}\) is the angle between \(\vec u\) and \(\vec v\text{.}\)
