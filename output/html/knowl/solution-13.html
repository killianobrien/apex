For a graphical approximation:
It appears that when \(x\) is close to \(1\text{,}\) that \({x^{3}+4x^{2}-4x+2}\) is close to \({3}\text{.}\) So \(\lim_{x\to 1}\left({x^{3}+4x^{2}-4x+2}\right)={3}\text{.}\)
