The density function \(\delta(x,y) = x^2+y^2+1\) becomes
\begin{equation*} \delta(r,\theta) = (r\cos(\theta) )^2+(r\sin(\theta) )^2+1 = r^2+1\text{.} \end{equation*}
The circle is bounded by \(0\leq r\leq 2\) and \(0\leq\theta\leq2\pi\text{.}\) Thus the weight \(W\) is:
\begin{align*} W \amp = \int_0^{2\pi}\int_0^2 (r^2+1)r\, dr\, d\theta\\ \amp = \int_0^{2\pi} \left.\left(\frac14r^4+\frac12r^2\right)\right|_0^2d\theta\\ \amp = \int_0^{2\pi} \left(6\right) d\theta\\ \amp = 12\pi \approx 37.70\text{ lb } \text{.} \end{align*}
