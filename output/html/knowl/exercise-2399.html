Exercise 38.
David faces Goliath with only a stone in a 3 ft sling, which he whirls above his head at 4 revolutions per second. They stand 20 ft apart.
  1. At what \(t\)-values must David release the stone in his sling in order to hit Goliath?
  2. What is the speed at which the stone is traveling when released?
  3. Assume David releases the stone from a height of 6ft and Goliath's forehead is 9 ft above the ground. What angle of elevation must David apply to the stone to hit Goliath's head?
