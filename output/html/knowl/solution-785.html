Solution 9.3.4.42.b.1.
For the first 12 terms, we take \(n=12\text{.}\) We find:
\begin{align*} \int_{12}^\infty \frac{1}{x^4}\,dx \amp = \frac{1}{5184} \approx 0.000193\\ \int_{13}^\infty \frac{1}{x^4}\,dx \amp = \frac{1}{6591} \approx 0.000152\\ \sum_{1}^{12}\frac{1}{n^4} \amp \approx 1.082153\text{.} \end{align*}
