We have
\begin{equation*} R_n\leq \int_n^\infty \frac{1}{x^4}\,dx = \frac{1}{3n^3}\text{.} \end{equation*}
For 5 decimal places of accuracy, we need \(R_n\leq 0.000005\text{.}\) Setting \(0.000005=1/(3n^3)\) and solving for \(n\) gives \(n\approx 40.55\text{,}\) so we should take 41 terms.
