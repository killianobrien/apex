Exercise 24.
A conical water tank is 5 m deep with a top radius of 3 m. (This is similar to Example 7.5.11.) The tank is filled with pure water, with a mass density of 1000 kg⁄m3.
  1. Find the work performed in pumping all the water to the top of the tank.
  2. Find the work performed in pumping the top 2.5 m of water to the top of the tank.
  3. Find the work performed in pumping the top half of the water, by volume, to the top of the tank.
