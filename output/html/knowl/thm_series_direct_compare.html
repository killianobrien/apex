Theorem 9.3.7. Direct Comparison Test.

Let \(\{a_n\}\) and \(\{b_n\}\) be positive sequences where \(a_n\leq b_n\) for all \(n\geq N\text{,}\) for some \(N\geq 1\text{.}\)
  1. If \(\ds \infser b_n\) converges, then \(\ds \infser a_n\) converges.
  2. If \(\ds \infser a_n\) diverges, then \(\ds \infser b_n\) diverges.

Proof.
