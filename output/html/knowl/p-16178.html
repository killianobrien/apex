We need to be careful to not “simplify” \(\snorm{\vec r_u\times \vec r_v} = 2\sqrt{6}\sqrt{(u-1)^2}\) as \(2\sqrt{6}(u-1)\text{;}\) rather, it is \(2\sqrt{6}|u-1|\text{.}\) In this example, \(u\) is bounded by \(0\leq u\leq 1\text{,}\) and on this interval \(|u-1| = 1-u\text{.}\) Thus \(dS = 2\sqrt{6}(1-u)dA\text{.}\)
