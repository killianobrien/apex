In the following exercises, find the area of the given surface over the region \(R\text{.}\)
11.
\(f(x,y) = 3x-7y+2\text{;}\) \(R\) is the rectangle with opposite corners \((-1,0)\) and \((1,3)\text{.}\)
12.
\(f(x,y) = 2x+2y+2\text{;}\) \(R\) is the triangle with corners \((0,0)\text{,}\) \((1,0)\) and \((0,1)\text{.}\)
13.
\(f(x,y) = x^2+y^2+10\text{;}\) \(R\) is bounded by the circle \(x^2+y^2=16\text{.}\)
14.
\(f(x,y) = -2x+4y^2+7\) over \(R\text{,}\) the triangle bounded by \(y=-x\text{,}\) \(y=x\text{,}\) \(0\leq y\leq 1\text{.}\)
15.
\(f(x,y) = x^2+y\) over \(R\text{,}\) the triangle bounded by \(y=2x\text{,}\) \(y=0\) and \(x=2\text{.}\)
16.
\(f(x,y) = \frac23x^{3/2}+2y^{3/2}\) over \(R\text{,}\) the rectangle with opposite corners \((0,0)\) and \((1,1)\text{.}\)
17.
\(f(x,y) = 10-2\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\) over the region \(R\) bounded by the circle \(x^2+y^2=25\text{.}\) (This is the cone with height 10 and base radius 5; be sure to compare your result with the known formula.)
18.
Find the surface area of the sphere with radius 5 by doubling the surface area of \(f(x,y) = \sqrt{25-x^2-y^2}\) over the region \(R\) bounded by the circle \(x^2+y^2=25\text{.}\) (Be sure to compare your result with the known formula.)
19.
Find the surface area of the ellipse formed by restricting the plane \(f(x,y) = cx+dy+h\) to the region \(R\) bounded by the circle \(x^2+y^2=1\text{,}\) where \(c\text{,}\) \(d\) and \(h\) are some constants. Your answer should be given in terms of \(c\) and \(d\text{;}\) why does the value of \(h\) not matter?
