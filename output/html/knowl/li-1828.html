Item 3.
\(\inflim \left(\frac{2^n}{n^2}\right)^{1/n} = \inflim \frac{2}{\big(n^{2/n}\big)} = 2\text{.}\) Since this is greater than 1, we conclude the series diverges. (Note: The Ratio Test is easy to apply to this series.)
