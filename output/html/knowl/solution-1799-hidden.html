Bounded below by the cone \(z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\) and above by the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2=4\text{:}\) describes a shape that is somewhat “diamond”-like; some think of it as looking like an ice cream cone (see Figure 14.7.25). It describes a cone, where the side makes an angle of \(\pi/4\) with the \(xy\)-plane, topped by the portion of the ball of radius 2, centered at the origin.
