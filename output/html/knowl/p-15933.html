\(\la 2\cos t,2\sin t\ra\text{,}\) \(0\leq t\leq 2\pi\text{.}\) The flux across \(C\) is
\begin{align*} \oint_C \vec F\cdot \vec n\, ds \amp = \oint_C\big(M\gp(t)-N\fp(t)\big)\, dt\\ \amp = \int_0^{2\pi} \big((2\cos t-2\sin t)(2\cos t) - (2\cos t+2\sin t)(-2\sin t)\big)\, dt\\ \amp = \int_0^{2\pi} 4\, dt = 8\pi\text{.} \end{align*}
