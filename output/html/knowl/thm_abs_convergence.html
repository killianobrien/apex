Theorem 9.5.13. Absolute Convergence Theorem.

Let \(\ds \infser a_n\) be a series that converges absolutely.
  1. \(\ds \infser a_n\) converges.
  2. Let \(\{b_n\}\) be any rearrangement of the sequence \(\{a_n\}\text{.}\) Then
    \begin{equation*} \infser b_n = \infser a_n\text{.} \end{equation*}

Proof.
