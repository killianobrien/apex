We test whether or not \(\crossp uv\) is orthogonal to \(\vec u\) and \(\vec v\) using the dot product:
\begin{equation*} \big(\crossp uv\big) \cdot \vec u = \la -13,2,7\ra \cdot \la 2,-1,4\ra = 0\text{,} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \big(\crossp uv\big) \cdot \vec v = \la -13,2,7\ra \cdot \la 3,2,5 \ra = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
