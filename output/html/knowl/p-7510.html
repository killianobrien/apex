The distance the water at height \(y\) travels is given by \(h(y)=3-y\text{.}\) Thus the total work done in pumping the water from the tank is
\begin{align*} W \amp = \int_{-10}^0 62.4\pi(1/5y+2)^2(3-y)\, dy\\ \amp = 62.4\pi\int_{-10}^0\left(-\frac1{25}y^3-\frac{17}{25}y^2-\frac85y+12\right)\, dy\\ \amp = 62.2\pi\cdot\frac{220}{3} \approx 14,376 \text{ft--lb}\text{.} \end{align*}
