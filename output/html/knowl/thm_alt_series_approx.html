Theorem 9.5.7. The Alternating Series Approximation Theorem.

Let \(\{a_n\}\) be a sequence that satisfies the hypotheses of the Alternating Series Test, and let \(S_n\) and \(L\) be the \(n\)th partial sums and sum, respectively, of either \(\ds \infser (-1)^{n}a_n\) or \(\ds \infser (-1)^{n+1}a_n\text{.}\) Then
  1. \(E_n=\abs{S_n-L} \lt a_{n+1}\text{,}\) and
  2. \(L\) is between \(S_n\) and \(S_{n+1}\text{.}\)
