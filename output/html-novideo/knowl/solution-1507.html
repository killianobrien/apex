Solution 14.7.3.20.1.
It is the region is space bounded below by \(z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\) and above by the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2=4\text{,}\) with the portion above the cone \(z=\sqrt3\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\) removed: it describes a cone, where the side makes an angle of \(\pi/4\) with the \(xy\)-plane, topped by the portion of the ball of radius 2, centered at the origin, with the inner cone with angle \(\pi/3\) removed, along with corresponding portion of the ball of radius 2.
