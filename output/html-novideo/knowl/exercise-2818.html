Exercise 7.
Bounds in cylindrical coordinates:
  1. \(r=1\text{,}\) \(0\leq \theta\leq 2\pi\text{,}\) \(0\leq z\leq 1\)
  2. \(1\leq r\leq 2\text{,}\) \(0\leq \theta\leq \pi\text{,}\) \(0\leq z\leq 1\)
Bounds in spherical coordinates:
  1. \(\rho=3\text{,}\) \(0\leq \theta\leq2\pi\text{,}\) \(0\leq\varphi\leq \pi/2\)
  2. \(2\leq\rho\leq3\text{,}\) \(0\leq \theta\leq2\pi\text{,}\) \(-\pi/2\leq\varphi\leq \pi/2\)
