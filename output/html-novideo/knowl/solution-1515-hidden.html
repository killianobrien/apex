In cylindrical coordinates, the density is \(\delta(r,\theta,z) = z\text{.}\) Thus mass is
\begin{equation*} M=\int_0^{2\pi}\int_2^3\int_0^{10} zr\, dz\, dr\, d\theta = 250\pi\text{.} \end{equation*}
We find \(M_{yz} = 0\text{,}\) \(M_{xz} = 0\text{,}\) and \(M_{xy} = 5000\pi/3\text{,}\) placing the center of mass at \((0,0,20/3)\text{.}\)
