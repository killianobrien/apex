Theorem 5.3.7 states \(\sum\limits_{i=1}^na_i = \sum\limits_{i=1}^k a_i + \sum\limits_{i=k+1}^n a_i\text{,}\) so \(\sum\limits_{i=k+1}^na_i = \sum\limits_{i=1}^n a_i - \sum\limits_{i=1}^k a_i\text{.}\) Use this fact, along with other parts of Theorem 5.3.7, to evaluate the summation.
