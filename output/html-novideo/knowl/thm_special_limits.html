Theorem 1.3.12. Special Limits.

  1. \(\displaystyle \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \dfrac{\sin(x)}{x} = 1\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \dfrac{\cos(x)-1}{x} = 0\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \lim\limits_{x\to 0} (1+x)^{1/x} = e\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \dfrac{e^x-1}{x} = 1\)
