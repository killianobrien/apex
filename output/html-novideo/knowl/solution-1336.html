Solution 13.8.3.16.1.
The region has two “corners” at \((1,1)\) and \((-1,1)\text{.}\)
Along \(y=1\text{,}\) there is no critical point.
Along \(y=x^2\text{,}\) there is a critical point at \((5/14,25/196)\approx (0.357,0.128)\text{.}\)
The function \(f\) itself has no critical points. Checking the value of \(f\) at the corners \((1,1)\text{,}\) \((-1,1)\) and the critical point \((5/14,25/196)\text{,}\) we find the absolute maximum is at \((5/14,25/196,25/28) \approx (0.357,0.128,0.893)\) and the absolute minimum is at \((-1,1,-12)\text{.}\)
