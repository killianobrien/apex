Computing the limit directly gives
\begin{align*} \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{f(2+h)-f(2)}{h} \amp = \lim_{h\to 0}\frac{-16(2+h)^2+150 - (-16(2)^2+150)}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0}\frac{-16(4+4h+h^2)+150 - 86}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0}\frac{-64-64h-16h^2+64}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0}\frac{-64h-16h^2}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0}(-64 -16h)\\ \amp =-64\text{.} \end{align*}
