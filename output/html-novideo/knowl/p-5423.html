The integrand looks similar to the derivative of the arctangent function. Note:
\begin{align*} \frac{1}{25+x^2} \amp = \frac{1}{25\left(1+\frac{x^2}{25}\right)}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{25(1+\left(\frac{x}{5}\right)^2)}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{25}\frac{1}{1+\left(\frac{x}{5}\right)^2}\text{.} \end{align*}
