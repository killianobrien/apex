Assume that Theorem 9.1.27 is true, and let \(\{a_n\}\) be bounded and monotonic. Since \(\{a_n\}\) is bounded, it is bounded both above and below. If it is increasing, it is bounded above and we apply Theorem 9.1.27; if it is decreasing, it is bounded below and we apply the theorem. Either way, \(\{a_n\}\) converges and the statement is true.
