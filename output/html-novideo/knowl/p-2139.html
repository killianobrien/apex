The derivative of \(y=\frac{1}{x^n}\) turned out to be rather nice. It gets better. Consider:
\begin{align*} \lzoo{x}{\frac{1}{x^n}} \amp = \lzoo{x}{x^{-n}} \amp\amp\text{ (apply result from } \knowl{./knowl/ex_deriv_power.html}{\text{Example 2.4.14}}\text{)}\\ \amp = -\frac{n}{x^{n+1}} \amp\amp \text{ (rewrite algebraically) }\\ \amp = -nx^{-(n+1)}\amp\\ \amp = -nx^{-n-1}\amp\text{.} \end{align*}
