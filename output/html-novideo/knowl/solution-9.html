For a graphical approximation:
It appears that when \(x\) is close to \(1\text{,}\) that \({x^{2}+2x+2}\) is close to \({5}\text{.}\) So \(\lim_{x\to 1}\left({x^{2}+2x+2}\right)={5}\text{.}\)
