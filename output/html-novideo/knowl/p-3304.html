A graph of \(S(t)\) and \(S'(t)\) is given in Figure 3.4.19. When \(S'(t)\lt 0\text{,}\) sales are decreasing; note how at \(t\approx 1.16\text{,}\) \(S'(t)\) is minimized. That is, sales are decreasing at the fastest rate at \(t\approx 1.16\text{.}\) On the interval of \((1.16,2)\text{,}\) \(S\) is decreasing but concave up, so the decline in sales is “leveling off.”
