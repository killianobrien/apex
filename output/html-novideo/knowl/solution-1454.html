Solution 14.5.6.1.
Analytically, \(g_x = 2f_x\) and \(g_y=2f_y\text{.}\) The double integral to compute the surface area of \(f\) over \(R\) is \(\ds \iint_R \sqrt{1+f_x^2+f_y^2}\, dA\text{;}\) the double integral to compute the surface area of \(g\) over \(R\) is \(\ds \iint_R\sqrt{1+4f_x^2+4f_y^2}\, dA\text{,}\) which is not twice the double integral used to calculate the surface area of \(f\text{.}\)
