Theorem 9.8.10. Algebra of Power Series.

Let \(\ds f(x) = \infser[0] a_nx^n\) and \(\ds g(x) = \infser[0] b_nx^n\) converge absolutely for \(\abs{x}\lt R\text{,}\) and let \(h(x)\) be a polynomial function.
  1. \(\ds f(x)\pm g(x) = \infser[0] (a_n\pm b_n)x^n\) for \(\abs{x}\lt R\text{.}\)
  2. \(\ds f(x)g(x) = \left(\infser[0] a_nx^n\right)\left(\infser[0] b_nx^n\right) = \infser[0]\big(a_0b_n+a_1b_{n-1}+\ldots a_nb_0\big)x^n\) for \(\abs{x}\lt R\text{.}\)
  3. \(\ds f\big(h(x)\big) = \infser[0] a_n\big(h(x)\big)^n\) for \(\abs{h(x)}\lt R\text{.}\)
