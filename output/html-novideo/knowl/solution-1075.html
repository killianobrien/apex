Solution 12.3.3.5.1.
One example is traveling at a constant speed \(s\) in a circle, ending at the starting position. Since the displacement is \(\vec 0\text{,}\) the average velocity is \(\vec 0\text{,}\) hence \(\vnorm 0=0\text{.}\) But traveling at constant speed \(s\) means the average speed is also \(s \gt 0\text{.}\)
