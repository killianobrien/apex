Theorem 9.2.17. Properties of Infinite Series.

Let \(\ds \infser a_n = L\text{,}\)\(\ds\infser b_n = K\text{,}\) and let \(c\) be a constant.
  1. Constant Multiple Rule: \(\ds\infser c\cdot a_n = c\cdot\infser a_n = c\cdot L\text{.}\)
  2. Sum/Difference Rule: \(\ds\infser \big(a_n\pm b_n\big) = \infser a_n \pm \infser b_n = L \pm K\text{.}\)
