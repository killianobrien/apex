We rewrite \(\frac12\cos^2(3\theta)\) as \(\frac14(1+\cos(6\theta))\text{.}\) We can also rewrite \(\frac13\cos^3(3\theta)\cos(\theta)\) as:
\begin{align*} \frac13\cos^3(3\theta)\cos(\theta) \amp = \frac13\cos^2(3\theta)\cos(3\theta)\cos(\theta)\\ \amp = \frac13\frac{1+\cos(6\theta)}2\big(\cos(4\theta)+\cos(2\theta)\big)\text{.} \end{align*}
