In the following exercises, a closed curve \(C\) that is the boundary of a surface \(\surfaceS\) is given along with a vector field \(\vec F\text{.}\) Verify Stokes' Theorem on \(C\text{;}\) that is, show \(\oint_C \vec F\cdot d\vec r = \iint_\surfaceS\big(\curl \vec F\,\big)\cdot\vec n\, dS\text{.}\)
