  1. Find \(\vec u+\vec v\text{,}\) \(\vec u-\vec v\text{,}\) \(2\vec u-3\vec v\text{.}\)

  3. Find \(\vec x\) where \(\vec u+\vec x = 2\vec v-\vec x\text{.}\)
