The above example may seem a little contrived. Another example demonstrating this important concept is \(f(x)= (\sin(x) )/x\text{.}\) We have considered this function several times in the previous sections. We found that \(\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin(x) }{x}=1\text{;}\) i.e., there is no vertical asymptote. No simple algebraic cancellation makes this fact obvious; we used the Theorem 1.3.8 in Section 1.3 to prove this.
