\(C\) is the curve whose \(x\) and \(y\) coordinates follow the parabola \(y=1-x^2\) from \(x=1\) to \(x=-1\text{,}\) then follow the line from \((-1,0)\) back to \((1,0)\text{,}\) where the \(z\) coordinates of \(C\) are determined by \(f(x,y) = 2x^2+y^2\text{,}\) and \(\surfaceS\) is the portion of \(z=2x^2+y^2\) enclosed by \(C\text{;}\) \(\vec F = \langle y^2+z,x,x^2-y\rangle\text{.}\)
