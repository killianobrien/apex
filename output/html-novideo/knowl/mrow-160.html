\begin{align*} \fp(x) \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{\frac{1}{x+h+1}-\frac{1}{x+1}}{h}\\ \end{align*}

Now find common denominator then subtract; pull \(1/h\) out front to facilitate reading.

\begin{align*} \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{1}{h}\cdot\left(\frac{x+1}{(x+1)(x+h+1)} - \frac{x+h+1}{(x+1)(x+h+1)}\right)\\ \end{align*}

Now simplify algebraically.

\begin{align*} \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac 1h\cdot\left(\frac{x+1-(x+h+1)}{(x+1)(x+h+1)}\right) \\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac1h\cdot\left(\frac{-h}{(x+1)(x+h+1)}\right)\\ \end{align*}

Finally, apply the limit.

\begin{align*} \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{-1}{(x+1)(x+h+1)}\\ \amp = \frac{-1}{(x+1)(x+1)}\\ \amp = \frac{-1}{(x+1)^2} \end{align*}
