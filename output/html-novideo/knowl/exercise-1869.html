Exercise 43.
Consider the ellipse given by \(\ds \frac{(x-1)^2}{4}+\frac{(y-3)^2}{12}=1\text{.}\)
  1. Verify that the foci are located at \((1,3\pm 2\sqrt{2})\text{.}\)
  2. The points \(P_1 = (2,6)\) and \(P_2 = (1+\sqrt{2},3+\sqrt{6}) \approx (2.414,5.449)\) lie on the ellipse. Verify that the sum of distances from each point to the foci is the same.
