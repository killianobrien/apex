The domain of \({\sqrt{1-x}}\) is \((-\infty,1]\text{,}\) and the domain of \({\sqrt{x+5}}\) is \([-5,\infty)\text{.}\) So the domain of \(f\) is \({\left[-5,1\right]}\text{.}\) And since \(f\) is the sum of two compositions of continuous functions, it is continuous on \({\left[-5,1\right]}\text{.}\)
