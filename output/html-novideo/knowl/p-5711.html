Add \(\ds\int e^x\cos(x)\, dx\) to both sides. This gives
\begin{align*} 2\int e^x\cos(x) \, dx \amp = e^x\sin(x) + e^x\cos(x)\\ \end{align*}

Now divide both sides by 2 and then add the integration constant:

\begin{align*} \int e^x\cos(x) \, dx \amp = \frac{1}{2}\big(e^x\sin(x) + e^x\cos(x) \big)+C\text{.} \end{align*}
