Solution 14.7.3.33.1.
In spherical coordinates, the density is \(\delta(\rho,\theta,\varphi) = \rho\sin(\varphi)\text{.}\) Thus mass is
\begin{equation*} \int_{\pi/4}^{\pi/2}\int_0^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{1} \big(\rho\sin(\varphi)\big)\rho^2\sin(\varphi)\, d\rho\, d\theta\, d\varphi = \pi/8\text{.} \end{equation*}
