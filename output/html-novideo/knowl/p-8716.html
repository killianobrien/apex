We compare the terms of \(\ds\infser \frac1{n+\ln(n) }\) to the terms of the Harmonic Sequence \(\ds\infser \frac1{n}\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1/(n+\ln(n) )}{1/n} \amp = \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{n}{n+\ln(n) }\\ \amp = 1 \text{ (after applying L'Hospital's Rule) } \text{.} \end{align*}
